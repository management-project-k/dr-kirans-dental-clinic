About Us

Our Services

Dental Implants

Advanced implant solutions for missing teeth.

What is a dental implant?

A titanium post surgically placed into the jawbone to support a crown.

Facial Trauma

Expert treatment for facial injuries and reconstruction.

How long does recovery take?

Typically 4-6 weeks, depending on severity.

Root Canal (RCT)

Painless root canal therapy to save your tooth.

Is it painful?

No, with modern anesthesia, it's comfortable.

Laser Dentistry

Precise, minimally invasive laser treatments.

What conditions does it treat?

Gum disease, cavities, and whitening.

Braces Adjustment

Orthodontic care for perfect alignment.

How often are adjustments needed?

Every 4-6 weeks.

Maxillofacial Prosthetics

Custom prosthetics for facial restoration.

Who needs this?

Patients with trauma or congenital defects.
