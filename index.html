Dental Services & Specialties

Dental Implants

Advanced implant solutions for missing teeth. Restore your confident smile!

What is a dental implant?

A titanium post surgically placed into the jawbone to support a crown.

Facial Trauma

Expert care and reconstruction for all facial injuries.

How long does recovery take?

Typically 4-6 weeks depending on severity.

Root Canal (RCT)

Painless root canal therapy to save and restore your tooth.

Is it painful?

No, with modern anesthesia, it's comfortable.

Laser Dentistry

Precise, minimally invasive laser dental treatments for gums and teeth.

What conditions does it treat?

Gum disease, cavities, and teeth whitening.

Braces & Orthodontics

Orthodontic care for perfect teeth alignment and jaw correction.

How often are adjustments needed?

Every 4-6 weeks for best results.

Maxillofacial Prosthetics

Custom prosthetics for facial and dental restoration after trauma or defects.

Who needs this?

Patients with trauma or congenital defects.
