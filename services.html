Our Dental Services

At Dr. Kiran’s Dental Center, we provide comprehensive dental and maxillofacial care with the latest technology and expert staff.

Dental Implants

Permanent solutions for missing teeth using biocompatible implants to restore your confident smile.

Facial Trauma

Expert repair and reconstruction for facial injuries to restore function and aesthetics quickly.

Root Canal (RCT)

Painless treatment to save infected teeth while preserving natural structure and health.

Laser Dentistry

Minimally invasive treatments for gum care, cavity preparation, and teeth whitening using lasers.

Braces & Orthodontics

Corrective orthodontic solutions for straight teeth and proper jaw alignment for all ages.

Maxillofacial Prosthetics

Custom prosthetics for jaw and facial reconstruction after trauma, surgery, or congenital conditions.
